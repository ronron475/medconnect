medConnect — PATIENT SAYINGS FOR TRAINING & DEMO
================================================================================
Use these when testing video calls, live transcription, and AI/ML analysis.
Doctor: open consultation_session.php → Start Video → Minimize video → Analyze Transcript
Patient: say the lines below (or read from phone) during the call.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
QUICK START (read these first — 5 easy lines)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Hiligaynon (fever + cough):
   "May hilanat kag ubo ako tatlo ka adlaw na. Nag-inom sang paracetamol."

2. Hiligaynon (stomach):
   "May sakit tiyan ako kag nagsuka. Wala ako gana magkaon."

3. English (chest — URGENT test):
   "I have chest pain and difficulty breathing since this morning."

4. Mixed (common teleconsult):
   "May hilanat kag ubo. I also feel tired and my head hurts."

5. Hiligaynon (allergy):
   "May sip-on ako, ginapanas-an, kag nagaluha ang mata. Daw may allergy."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FOR LIVE VIDEO CALL — say slowly, pause 3 seconds between sentences
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SCRIPT A — Common cold / flu style
  "Doctor, may hilanat ako."
  "May ubo man kag sip-on."
  "Ginakapoy ako permi."
  "Wala ako gana magkaon."

SCRIPT B — Stomach / GERD style
  "May sakit tiyan ako human magkaon."
  "Nagsuka ako kag may acidity."
  "May hapdi man sa dughan gamay lang."

SCRIPT C — Urgent (triage should flag HIGH)
  "May sakit dughan ako."
  "Budlay ang ginhawa ko."
  "May hilanat man."

SCRIPT D — Diabetes / fatigue style (English)
  "I feel very tired all day."
  "I am thirsty often and urinate frequently."
  "I have lost some weight."

SCRIPT E — Skin / rash style
  "May katol ako kag may rashes sa panit."
  "Red patches on my skin for one week."

ENGLISH LINES BY CONDITION (for ML training test)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  "I have been sneezing, shivering, and my eyes are watering."

  "I am always tired, thirsty, and urinating more than usual."

MIGRAINE:
  "Severe headache, nausea, and pain behind my eyes."

HYPERTENSION:
  "Headache, chest pain, and dizziness. My blood pressure feels high."

URINARY TRACT INFECTION:
  "Burning when I urinate and pain in my lower abdomen."
